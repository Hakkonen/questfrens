FAQ
  • Q: How do I mint?

    A: First you need to acquire a Questfren mint token, this is any QUESTFREN subasset that has not been minted yet. Dispensers for them can be found here:
    Market
    Then you need to broadcast a signature message in Freewallet. This is in the format as follows:

    MINT QUESTFREN number optional_name

    number: is your Questfren asset number
    optional_name is an optional name. It cannot be a repeated name, and must be under 15 characters.

  • Q: Why is an asset not appearing when I broadcast?

    A: The mint contract will only recognise the address that holds that number mint token. Counterparty tokens require XCP to mint, so it cannot be done by broadcasting alone.

  • Q: How does the generation work?

    A: When you send a broadcast on the XCP network, your bitcoin private key signs the message. This one-way encryption allows the network to verify that you, and only you, are ready to mint.
    If someone else sends a message trying to mint your token it will not work, because the contract will not recognise a signature from an address that does not hold the mint token.

  • Q: Do I have to mint my Questfren now?

    A: No, you can mint your token whenever you want. Or trade it whenver you want.

  • Q: Do I have to include a name?

    A: No. You can leave your name field blank.

  • Q: What are the limits for names?

    A: Names can be 15 characters or less, and cannot include spaces. If someone else has already used that names, your name choice will be invalid.
